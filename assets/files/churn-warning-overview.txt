Churn Early Warning System - Case Notes
---------------------------------------
Objective:
Provide customer success with forward-looking churn signals so action plans can be prioritised before renewals.

Highlights:
- Binary churn classification layered with cohort trend visuals.
- DAX pattern uses CALCULATE with time intelligence to compare risk evolution week over week.
- Integrated Teams alerts trigger when an account exceeds the risk tolerance threshold.

Usage tips:
- Start with the Risk Overview page to scan customer health segments.
- Drill into Playbooks to understand recommended retention motions.
- Filter by segment or CSM owner to personalise follow-up plans.
