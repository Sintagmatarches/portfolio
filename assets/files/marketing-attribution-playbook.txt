Marketing Attribution Tracker - Optimisation Playbook
-----------------------------------------------------
Use this guide to interpret the Power BI report and activate winning acquisition tactics.

Insights included:
- Spend vs. pipeline velocity matrix exposes channels that need nurturing or investment.
- Incrementality models combine first-touch and multi-touch credit to estimate true ROI.
- What-if parameter controls allow scenario planning for budget shifts across quarters.

Recommended workflow:
1. Review the "Cohort ROI" page to identify campaigns exceeding CAC targets.
2. Use the "Lead Quality" tab to compare conversion rates across key segments.
3. Export the "Action Queue" to assign next steps to marketing owners.
