Retail Footfall & Conversion Command Center
==========================================

Key questions answered
----------------------
- Which stores and dayparts convert traffic most efficiently?
- How do weather, promotions, and staffing interplay with conversions?
- What staffing plan closes the gap to weekly targets?

Dataset notes
-------------
- Source systems: POS, IoT door counters, workforce management, promotion calendar
- Grain: Store-day-hour with incremental refresh and dataflows for cleansing
- Sensitive fields redacted; replace with your own lookups before sharing externally

How to use this file
--------------------
1. Open the PBIX and refresh against your data gateway.
2. Review the "Scenario Planner" page to simulate labor adjustments.
3. Export the "Exec Snapshot" to PDF for stakeholders each Monday.
