Subscription Revenue Intelligence
=================================

Summary
-------
- Audience: SaaS leadership team (ARR $40M)
- Objective: unify CRM, billing, and product usage metrics to monitor recurring revenue health
- Impact: eliminated 18 manual slide decks per quarter and unlocked proactive churn prevention workflows

Highlights
----------
1. Data model blends Salesforce, Stripe, and Product telemetry in a star schema with incremental refresh.
2. Dynamic cohort visuals allow executives to compare plan upgrades vs. downgrades across 12 month windows.
3. DAX measures for leading indicators (activation velocity, logo retention) feed anomaly detection logic.
