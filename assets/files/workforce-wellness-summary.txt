Workforce Wellness Pulse - Executive Summary
-------------------------------------------
Mission:
Give HR and People Ops a real-time pulse on morale, workload balance, and burnout risk.

Dashboard features:
- Sentiment analysis from survey verbatims summarised with AI-driven keywords.
- Absenteeism heatmap by department layered with productivity signals and overtime.
- Power Automate flows send wellbeing nudges and collect follow-up feedback.

Actions:
- Monitor the "Wellness Score" trend for significant dips and coordinate outreach.
- Use the "Engagement Drivers" visual to discover positive or negative themes.
- Share the "Insights Recap" PDF export after monthly leadership reviews.
