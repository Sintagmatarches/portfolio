Case Study Report | BigQuery & Power BI | 2026

E-commerce Funnel Analysis (theLook Dataset)

End-to-end investigation of an 840,000+ event e-commerce dataset across product, cart, and purchase stages using Google BigQuery and Power BI. Identified that the primary volume drop occurs during checkout rather than initial cart addition, and analyzed geographical loss concentration across 10+ major markets and high-volume urban segments.

  • Dataset: 843,608 product events, 593,628 cart additions, 181,420 purchases
  • Key Finding: Largest absolute drop (412,208 events) happens between cart and purchase (30.56% conversion from cart)
  • Geographic Analysis: Evaluated volume ranking across China, US, and Brazil, plus city-level loss concentration (Shanghai, Beijing, Seoul)
  • Methodology: Event-level ratio comparison, traffic source distribution checks, and next-step conversion scoping
